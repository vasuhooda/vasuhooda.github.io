About Me


I am a 2021 PhD candidate in the Department of Earth, Environment and Planetary Sciences at Rice University, Houston. Currently, I work on early Solar System processes with Dr Rajdeep Dasgupta's group at Rice. In 2019, I received the Future Investigators in NASA Earth and Space Science and Technology (FINESST) grant for two years.


I also received the Innovation in Science Pursuit for Inspired Research (INSPIRE), Department of Science and Technology, India in 2007 for a period of five years. As an INSPIRE scholar, I obtained the dual degree of Bachelor of Science and Master of Science in Geology from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur in 2012. After graduation, I taught high school level chemistry to children for three years in my hometown, Patiala (Punjab), India.
